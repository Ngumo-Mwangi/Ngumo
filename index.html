ABOUT ME

Creative Technologist | Entrepreneur | Innovator

Ngumo Mwangi is a multidisciplinary creator, entrepreneur, and creative technologist driven by innovation, artistic expression, and community development. His work sits at the intersection of technology, art, fashion, music, and business, where he transforms ideas into impactful projects and creative ventures.

Raised in Dallas, Embu, Kenya, Ngumo’s early life was deeply rooted in farming, community collaboration, and entrepreneurial thinking. Growing up in an environment that required discipline and resilience, he learned the value of consistent effort, responsibility, and adaptability. Early mornings tending livestock, helping with farming activities, and balancing school responsibilities shaped a mindset centered on perseverance, hard work, and practical problem solving.

The rhythm of rural life taught him patience and long-term thinking, while strong community bonds instilled values of teamwork, leadership, and mutual support. These early experiences became the foundation for his ability to lead projects, innovate creatively, and build ventures that combine culture, technology, and entrepreneurship.

Ngumo began his academic journey at St. Michael Primary School, where he developed strong discipline and curiosity for learning. He later attended Embu Urban Integrated Secondary School, where he strengthened his leadership skills, critical thinking, and resilience through academic and extracurricular activities.

He continued his education at Jomo Kenyatta University of Agriculture and Technology, where he expanded his expertise across technology, agriculture, creative arts, and information technology. His university experience refined his skills in project management, research, digital innovation, and professional collaboration.

Throughout his career, Ngumo has built and contributed to several ventures that reflect his diverse creative and technical interests.

At Exquisite Tatu Nation, he leads creative projects within a tattoo studio that blends artistic expression with modern digital branding and cultural identity. His work includes designing tattoos, mentoring artists, and coordinating creative media and artistic direction.

Through Intenzionale Fashion, he contributes to the development of a fashion brand focused on creative streetwear and artistic identity. His responsibilities include design development, production oversight, marketing strategies, and e-commerce operations.

In the events and entertainment industry, Ngumo has worked with Audio Mix Sound Center, where he manages sound engineering systems, supports live event production, and implements technical solutions for professional audio experiences.

He is also involved in Ujenzi Art Shop, where he facilitates creative workshops, supports artists, and manages artistic and digital projects that empower creativity within the community.

Beyond creative industries, Ngumo maintains a strong connection to agriculture through GKM Farming and Produce, where he participates in crop planning, livestock management, and agricultural production. His work with Mwanzi Lisi Building and Construction further demonstrates his involvement in operational coordination and construction project support.

Ngumo’s skillset spans multiple creative and technical disciplines, including drawing, tattoo artistry, graphic design, video creation, songwriting, fashion design, audio engineering, information technology, event production, farming techniques, and project management. In addition to technical skills, he emphasizes leadership, mentorship, public speaking, teamwork, and community organization.

At the core of his philosophy is the belief that creativity and innovation should not only produce art but also create opportunities and empower communities. His projects reflect a vision where technology, culture, and entrepreneurship come together to build meaningful impact.

Guided by the bold motto displayed on his platform — “If You’re Not Loud, You’re Not Allowed.” — Ngumo embraces fearless creativity, expressive ideas, and forward-thinking innovation as tools for shaping the future.
